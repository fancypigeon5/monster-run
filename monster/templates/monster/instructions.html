Instructions

Creating monsters

Start the game by creating a monster.

Monster Page

On the monster page you will see a list of all the monsters you own, displayed with their stats and equipped equipment (if htey have any).

Equipment Page

On the equipment page you can manage your equipments. Click on any not owned equipments, they will then appear in the unlocking section. After having made enough progress by entering running data you will see a button appear to unlock them. After unlocking them they appear in the owned section. When clicking on them now a prompt appears asking to select the monster to equip it to. After equipping it they appear next to the monster they were equipped to (each monster can have 1 weapon and one armour). Clicking again will Unequip them and move them back to owned.

Run data

Here you can enter your runs. When entering a run, choose whether you want to use it to heal a monster or to unlock equipment. If a run was entered wrong, you can delete it (this will also delete the benefits for healing or unlocking this run made).

Arena

Here you can fight monsters to gain points. Or you can access the Scoreboard to see the monsters with the most points.
